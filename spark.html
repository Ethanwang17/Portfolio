The Application Page

Spark • Jun 2023 - Aug 2023

For this project, we were tasked with constructing a website that functions as the primary platform for potential future interns to learn more about Spark and submit their applications. Additionally, interns will engage in collaborative efforts to create an advanced admin panel strategically designed to provide recruiters with efficient tools to assess and oversee applicant data.

1. Define

Problem

The problem is that previously, the only method of application was a Google Form, which presented challenges in terms of user experience and accessibility. The existing process lacked an intuitive and branded interface, resulting in user confusion and potential drop-offs. Transitioning to a dedicated application page presents an opportunity to address these issues, creating a seamless, visually appealing, and user-centric experience that enhances engagement and ensures a positive impression of the Spark Internship Program.

User Research

User Interviews: Conducting one-on-one user interviews allowed me to delve deep into the minds of prospective interns. I crafted interview scripts that probed into their challenges, preferences, and frustrations during the application process. Through these candid conversations, I unearthed nuanced insights that guided our design decisions, ensuring we addressed specific pain points such as complex forms, unclear instructions, and technical glitches. The qualitative nature of these interviews provided invaluable context to the quantitative data collected through surveys.



Surveys: To validate and quantify the trends identified during user interviews, I crafted and distributed surveys to a wider audience. These surveys covered a range of topics, from the clarity of application criteria to the design preferences for the application page. The survey data offered a broader perspective, enabling us to pinpoint common trends and prioritize design solutions that would cater to a larger applicant base. I meticulously analyzed the survey responses, identifying key areas for improvement and generating data-backed insights.

Key Insights

Simplicity Drives Engagement
